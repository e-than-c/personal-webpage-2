Workplace Solutions - Accessibility Status of Data Assets in CDP 2.0

This interactive dashboard is designed to enhance data transparency and support access governance within the Workplace Solutions team. It provides a comprehensive overview of over 1,700 data assets, highlighting their accessibility status based on classification logic and ownership rules.

Link to open in new tab here

Impact

This dashboard has transformed data transparency and access governance for CDP 2.0 users by providing a centralized view of over 1,700 data assets. Previously, users lacked a clear way to determine which data assets were accessible or how to request access, relying solely on messaging the Data Governance team, which was a process that was inefficient and time-consuming. By clearly highlighting the accessibility status of each asset, the dashboard has streamlined the access request process, reducing confusion, improving efficiency, and providing a more transparent and user-friendly system. Furthermore, the dashboard was later expanded to support four additional business areas, providing visibility into over 4,000 data assets and further accelerating access processes across the organization.

Impact

The development of these dashboards has enabled leadership to implement more effective communication strategies. For instance, email campaigns are now scheduled in the morning (around 9-10 AM) to maximize engagement and open rates. Additionally, the dashboards provided visibility into the number of events held across data-focused Communities of Practice, which previously operated independently without centralized tracking. This newfound transparency led to collaborative efforts among community leaders to increase the number of events, particularly during the summer, promoting greater engagement and collaboration.
