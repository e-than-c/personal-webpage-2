FitBook

Social media app that combines fitness tracking with social connectivity.

Introduction

FitBook is a unique social media platform that integrates fitness tracking with social connectivity. Users can share their fitness journeys, track their progress, and connect with friends and like-minded individuals.

Features

  • Track workouts and fitness progress
  • Share updates and achievements with friends
  • Join fitness challenges and groups
  • Connect with fitness enthusiasts

Technologies Used

  • Design: Figma
  • Frontend: React Native
  • Backend: Node.js, Express.js
  • Database: MongoDB
